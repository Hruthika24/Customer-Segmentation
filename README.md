# Customer Segmentation Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer demographics and purchasing behavior using Power BI. Customers are segmented into different groups based on Annual Income and Spending Score to identify purchasing patterns and customer preferences.

## Objectives

* Segment customers based on behavior and demographics
* Analyze customer spending patterns
* Visualize customer segments and key characteristics
* Generate business insights for targeted marketing

## Dataset Features

* Customer ID
* Age
* Gender
* City
* Annual Income
* Spending Score
* Purchase Amount
* Region

## Data Cleaning

* Removed duplicate records
* Handled missing values
* Standardized gender values

## Customer Segments

* High Income High Spending
* High Income Low Spending
* Low Income High Spending
* Low Income Low Spending

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards
* Income vs Spending Scatter Plot
* Customer Segment Distribution
* Gender Distribution
* Purchase Amount by Region
* Purchase Amount by City
* Interactive Filters and Slicers

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel

## Key Insights

* Largest customer segment: Low Income Low Spending (43%)
* Vizag has the highest purchase amount
* East region leads in purchases
* Male and Female customers are nearly equal

## Outcome

Developed practical skills in customer analytics, segmentation, data cleaning, dashboard creation, and business intelligence reporting.
